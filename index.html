Tabaarak Trust Aid Foundation (TTAF)

Tabaarak Trust Aid Foundation.(TTAF) is an international humanitarian organization founded in 15 JAN, 2015 with the aim of fostering and contributing to humanitarian efforts in the African continent with particular focus on Somalia. The organization provides emergency relief and training that are more resilient and innovative in nature to communities affected by natural disasters such as the recurring droughts and floods, diseases as well as ethnic and religious conflicts. The organization also acts as a think-tank body to public ally discuss the dynamics of droughts, floods and conflicts affecting the continent and Somalia in particular and conduct research that would guide policy makers and stakeholders to lay down measures to manage the aforementioned challenges in their respective countries or regions
